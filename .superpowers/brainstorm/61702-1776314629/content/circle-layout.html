Circle of Fifths — Visual Structure

How should the circle and its information be arranged on the page?

▸ Key of C Major — Relative minor: Am — Dominant: G

A — Circle Focused

Large circle dominates the page. Info panel below or overlaid when a key is selected. Clean, immersive — the circle IS the page.

B — Circle + Sidebar

Circle on the left, detail panel on the right. Room for key info, chord lists, and mode toggles (reference/explore/teach) in the sidebar.

C — Tabs + Floating Info

Mode tabs (Explore / Reference / Learn) at top. Circle dominates. Key info appears as floating tooltip near the selected segment. Minimal chrome.
